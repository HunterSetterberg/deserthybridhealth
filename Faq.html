Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’ve got answers.

Do I need to live in a specific location?

Nope. Desert Hybrid Health is 100% online. As long as you have internet access and a willingness to take action, we can work with you from anywhere.

Do you accept insurance?

We do not bill insurance. This allows us to focus fully on personalized, proactive care without the limitations of traditional insurance models. You'll know exactly what you're paying for — no surprise bills.

What does the program include?

You’ll get a personalized training plan, nutrition guidance, and access to a physician who monitors your health and helps guide your long-term wellness — all coordinated for your goals.

What’s the commitment?

Our programs are month-to-month, with no long-term contracts. We want you to stay because it’s working — not because you're locked in.

Is this right for me if I’m not an athlete?

Absolutely. While we do work with high performers, our system is designed for anyone who wants to take control of their health — whether you’re just getting started or already training hard.

How do I get started?

Start by joining the waitlist. We’ll reach out with next steps as we prepare for our January 2026 launch.
